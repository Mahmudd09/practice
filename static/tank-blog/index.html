History of Tanks

The history of tanks begins with World War I, when armoured all-terrain fighting vehicles were introduced as a response to the problems of trench warfare, ushering in a new era of mechanized warfare. Tanks were developed separately and simultaneously by Great Britain and France as a means to break the deadlock of trench warfare on the Western Front
